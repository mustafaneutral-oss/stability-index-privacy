# Privacy Policy — Stability Index

**Last updated: [30-05-2026]**

This Privacy Policy describes how Stability Index handles user information.

## What we collect

**The app does not collect any personal data.** All game data — saves, settings, language preference, achievements, progress — is stored locally on your device. There is no account system, no sign-in, and no transmission of gameplay data to any server.

## Third-party services

The app uses these third-party services that may collect limited information:

### Google AdMob (advertising)
Stability Index shows rewarded video ads when you choose to unlock a country. Google AdMob may collect: device advertising ID, IP address, device model, operating system version, and ad interaction events. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings (Settings → Google → Ads).

AdMob privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### Google Play Billing (in-app purchases)
If you purchase "Unlock all countries" ($2.99), payment is processed by Google Play. The app receives only a confirmation of purchase. No payment details are stored by the app.

Google Play privacy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Children's privacy

The app is intended for users aged 13 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Parents or guardians who believe their child has provided personal data should contact us.

## Data storage

All gameplay data is stored locally via AsyncStorage on your device. Uninstalling the app deletes all local data. There is no cloud sync of save files.

## Your rights

The app does not collect personal data, so there is no personal data for the developer to access or delete. For data collected by Google AdMob or Google Play, refer to Google's privacy controls.

## Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated occasionally. Updates will be reflected by changing the "Last updated" date above.
